# V_PL Video Analytics

Use this skill for analytics requirements and concepts.

## Analytics Layers

- Orthophoto generation from video or frame sets, with possible Metashape
  processing.
- Orthophoto publication and review through GeoServer or compatible GIS layers.
- Last high-quality frame with the target object, stored separately for rapid
  review.
- Orthophoto stitching across missions or time windows.
- Geolocation of frames, objects, route points, and annotations.
- Terrain/object change analysis across orthophoto time series.
- Real-time operator annotations: object labels, friendly product markers,
  target direction, and contextual warnings.

## Boundaries

Do not imply that analytics output is authoritative without confidence,
timestamp, source, and review status. Keep raw video and derived analytics
records traceable back to product and time.
